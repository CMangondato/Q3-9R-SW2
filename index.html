We Bare Bears

Characters:

Information:

Ice Bear

His body is completely covered in white fur. Even though he is the youngest of the Bears, he is taller, thinner, and stronger than his older brothers.

Grizzly

Grizzly is portrayed as outgoing, boisterous, friendly and outdoorsy. Out of the Three Bears, he stands out the most and sets good examples for them. He's always optimistic and gluttonous.

Panda

Panda is somewhat jumpy and insecure. He is probably the nervous, frail and shyest of the three bears. He is also severely allergic to nuts and gets embarrassed easily.
